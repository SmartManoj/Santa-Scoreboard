About the Competition

The Santa 2025 - Christmas Tree Packing Challenge is a Kaggle competition focused on optimizing the placement and orientation of Christmas trees in a constrained space. This competition features 200 tasks, each requiring strategic placement and rotation of items to maximize the overall score.
